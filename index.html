Known as burek in Slavic countries, boureki or spanakopita in Greece, bourekas in Isreal, or boureg in Armenian, böreks are truly an international dish.

Börek, originating from Ottoman Cuisine, in the Turkish language refers to any dish made with a round sheeted dough called yufka.
