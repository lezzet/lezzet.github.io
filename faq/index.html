FAQ

Where are the böreks made?

All Lezzet Börek's are made in a private kitchen in New Jersey by a Turkish family. We handmake all our böreks, including the yufka and phyllo dough, with organic ingredients.

 

What does Lezzet mean?

Lezzet, our company name, means "delicious taste" in Turkish. The name reflects our product very well! We are sure you will fall head over heals for our delicious böreks.

 

Why should I buy böreks?

There are so many reasons! First off, if you've never tried one before, you must try it at least once! It is a dish common in many parts of the world and is an excellent way to immerse yourself to different cultures and expand your palette. Second of all, they are filling! The beauty of the börek is how versatile it is: you can have it as a late-night snack or a meal. Either way, theres no need to feel guilty because it is a healthy treat.

 

Can I sell böreks in my store or buy them wholesale?

ABSOLUTELY! Ordering 14 makes a typical tray of böreks. Feel free to order on our online store or email us at wholesale@lezzetboreks.com with your business information and we'll certainly guide you through the best option for you!

 

How can I make a bigger order for a party or special event?

We provide the option to buy our böreks in trays on our online store. If you would like to curate a specific order and need guidance, feel free to email us at info@lezzetboreks.com.

 

Shipping and
delivery

Lezzet offers international shipping. Shipping in America can take up to 7 business days. We filfill your order by making them fresh and hot the minute you place your order. We're unable to deliver on public holidays so please allow an extra business day. We will provide you with a tracking number, once your order has been shipped.

International deliveries via UPS can take up to 10 business days, sometimes more… so be sure to keep your tracking number handy. Should you require any further assistance contact us at shipping@lezzetboreks.com.

Please note that international customers are responsible for any additional import fees or customs charges at the point of delivery. Sadly, we have no say in customs charges, duties, or taxes as they are assessed individually and vary by country. We suggest contacting your local customs office before placing your order, if more information is needed.

Item care

To remain fresh, keep your böreks refrigerated! Follow the instructions on the packaging of the börek for more specific ways to prepare them. Generally, placing the börek on a stove top, microwave, or oven for a couple minutes is all you need.

All of our products are securely packed before shipping.
