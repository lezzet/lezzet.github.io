Description

Tray Böreks, made in house with homemade phyllo dough, are hand wrapped into triangles the size of your palm in a big tray (hence, the name). They make for an excellent healthy snack or meal (really depends on your mood).

Depending on your taste, pick one of the fillings on the side!

One order consists of 2 böreks. Roughly 7 orders (14 böreks) is one tray.

Microwave or put it in the oven for about 5 minutes. More specific instructions are inside the package
